# Tonga Prompts

This directory contains AI prompts specifically designed for the Tongan context, focusing on the Kakala methodology, Faka'apa'apa (respect), and the relational ethics of Tongan culture.

## Prompts Available
- **[General Review Prompt](general-review-prompt.md)**: Use this for general documents, products, or policies.
- **[Workplace Review Prompt](workplace-review-prompt.md)**: Use this when you have an internal Tongan or Pacific engagement strategy to align with.

## Key Frameworks Referenced
- **Kakala**: A research and engagement methodology based on the process of making a garland (fragrance, beauty, and utility).
- **Faka'apa'apa**: The foundational value of respect in Tongan culture.
- **Anga Faka-Tonga**: The Tongan way of life, values, and protocols.
- **Relational Ethics**: Ensuring that engagement is grounded in reciprocity and long-term benefit.

## Regional Context
These prompts acknowledge the unique dynamics of both the Kingdom of Tonga and the Tongan diaspora, ensuring that engagement is grounded in respect, reciprocity, and the "fragrance" of lasting community benefit.
