# First Nations, Métis, and Inuit (Canada) Prompts

This directory contains AI prompts specifically designed for the Canadian context, focusing on the TRC Calls to Action, OCAP® principles, and distinction-based rights.

## Prompts Available
- **[General Review Prompt](general-review-prompt.md)**: Use this for general documents, products, or policies.
- **[Workplace Review Prompt](workplace-review-prompt.md)**: Use this when you have an internal TRC Action Plan or Indigenous Engagement Strategy to align with.

## Key Frameworks Referenced
- **TRC Calls to Action**: The 94 calls from the Truth and Reconciliation Commission.
- **OCAP® Principles**: Ownership, Control, Access, and Possession of Indigenous data.
- **UNDRIP / Bill C-15**: The UN Declaration and its implementation in Canadian law.
- **Section 35**: Constitutional recognition of Aboriginal and Treaty rights.

## Regional Context
These prompts are "Distinction-Based," ensuring that the unique rights and protocols of First Nations, Métis, and Inuit peoples are not blurred into a single category.
