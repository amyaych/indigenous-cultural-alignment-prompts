# Samoa Prompts

This directory contains AI prompts specifically designed for the Samoan context, focusing on the Fa'amatai (chiefly system), the Va (relational space), and the relational ethics of Fa'asamoa.

## Prompts Available
- **[General Review Prompt](general-review-prompt.md)**: Use this for general documents, products, or policies.
- **[Workplace Review Prompt](workplace-review-prompt.md)**: Use this when you have an internal Samoan or Pacific engagement strategy to align with.

## Key Frameworks Referenced
- **The Va**: The relational space between people, land, and the spiritual.
- **Fa'amatai**: The traditional chiefly governance system of Samoa.
- **Teu le va**: The concept of "nurturing the relational space" between people and groups.
- **Fa'asamoa**: The Samoan way of life, values, and protocols.

## Regional Context
These prompts acknowledge the unique dynamics of both the Islands of Samoa and the Samoan diaspora, ensuring that engagement is grounded in respect, reciprocity, and the nurturing of relational spaces.
