# Fiji Prompts

This directory contains AI prompts specifically designed for the Fijian context, focusing on the Vanua, traditional leadership, and the relational ethics of the Pacific.

## Prompts Available
- **[General Review Prompt](general-review-prompt.md)**: Use this for general documents, products, or policies.
- **[Workplace Review Prompt](workplace-review-prompt.md)**: Use this when you have an internal Fijian or Pacific engagement strategy to align with.

## Key Frameworks Referenced
- **The Vanua**: The interconnectedness of land, people, culture, and spirituality.
- **Traditional Leadership**: Respecting the role of Chiefs and community structures.
- **Sea of Islands**: Epeli Hau'ofa's framework for Oceanic identity and regionalism.
- **Talanoa**: Inclusive, participatory, and transparent dialogue.

## Regional Context
These prompts acknowledge the unique dynamics of both the Vanua in Fiji and the Fijian diaspora, ensuring that engagement is grounded in respect and reciprocity.
