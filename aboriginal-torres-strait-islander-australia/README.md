# Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander (Australia) Prompts

This directory contains AI prompts specifically designed for the Australian context, focusing on Country-centred design, Elder authority, and Reconciliation frameworks.

## Prompts Available
- **[General Review Prompt](general-review-prompt.md)**: Use this for general documents, products, or policies.
- **[Workplace Review Prompt](workplace-review-prompt.md)**: Use this when you have an internal Reconciliation Action Plan (RAP) to align with.

## Key Frameworks Referenced
- **Uluru Statement from the Heart**: Voice, Treaty, and Truth.
- **UNDRIP**: United Nations Declaration on the Rights of Indigenous Peoples.
- **Closing the Gap**: National agreement on socio-economic outcomes.
- **AIATSIS Code of Ethics**: For research and engagement.

## Regional Context
These prompts emphasise the distinction between Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander cultures and the importance of recognising the specific Traditional Owners and Custodians of the Country.
